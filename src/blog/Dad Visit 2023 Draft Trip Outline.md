- [Rijksmuseum Tickets](https://www.tiqets.com/en/amsterdam-attractions-c75061/tickets-for-rijksmuseum-skip-the-ticket-line-p703796/?selected_date=2023-09-28&selected_timeslot_id=4298500)
  - Best to go early morning or late afternoon.
- [Canal Boat Rental Site](https://booking.sloepdelen.nl/en/book/where-when?location=12&date=2023-09-29&onlyLocations=1,12,11)
  - Great for a 2 hour lazy boat tour of Amsterdam canals, can bring snacks and beverages.
- [Molen Van Sloten Windmill](https://molenvansloten.nl/)
  - The mill is open daily from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Guided tours every hour, the first at 10am and the last at 4pm. Access only with guided tour of approx. 45 minutes.
- [Gooi Natuurreservaat - Bussummerheide - Large Heathland](https://gnr.nl/gebieden/bussummerheide/)
  - Big nature reserve surrounding us in Bussum, great for walking.
- [Cafe Heidezicht on the Bussumerheide](https://heidezicht.com/)
  - Cafe that sits right on the heathland, also has a playground for kids and a bunch of weird animals for some reason. Albino wallabies!
- [Atlas Obscura - 66 Cool, Hidden, and Unusual Things to Do in Amsterdam, Netherlands](https://www.atlasobscura.com/things-to-do/amsterdam-netherlands)
- [Atlas Obscura - 311 Cool, Hidden, and Unusual Things to Do in the Netherlands](https://www.atlasobscura.com/things-to-do/netherlands)
- [Amsterdam Bos](https://www.amsterdamsebos.nl/)
  - Huge park in Amsterdam, has a working farm and a ton of nature stuff.
